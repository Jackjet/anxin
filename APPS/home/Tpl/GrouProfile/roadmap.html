2012

 In July, this group acquired Dajia Co., Ltd as well as its wholly-owned subsidiary, Jilin Yingke Information Technology Co., Ltd, to strengthen the position of China’s Largest ISD Integrated Solutions Provider.

2012

 The group signed the stock purchase & service agreement with Mango, Infinity and Mate Intelligent Video Ltd, and acquired all home equity of Anxin Mate Holdings Limited to promote the product business of this group.

2011

 In October, the group acquired all home equity of both Yuhong Investment Co., Ltd and its wholly-owned subsidiary, Shenzhen Hawell Future Technology, to promote the product business of this group.

2010

 The group was formally renamed as “Anxin-China Holdings Limited”.

2010

 The group decided to adjust the development strategies, for instance, focusing resources in developing the business of high-tech intelligent security precaution system and exiting the business of medicine manufacture and sales.

2009

 In October, the group acquired Xiangying Co., Ltd while still holding all ownership equity of Shenzhen An-Xin Digital Development Co., Ltd, which signed that it entered the international security market successfully.

2008

 Safety Production Real-time Remote Surveillance System, developed by Shenzhen An-Xin Digital Development Co., Ltd, was awarded the State’s Invention Patent Certificate.

2004

 Emergency Rescue Command & Coordination System, developed by Shenzhen An-Xin Digital Development Co., Ltd, passed the technical appraisal of the State Administration of Work Safety.

2003

 The group went public on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange, with its main business in medicine manufacture and sales.

1997

 Shenzhen An-Xin Digital Development Co., Ltd was formally founded.

 本集團與Mango、Infinity 及Mate Intelligent Video Ltd.簽署購股及服務協議，收購目標公司Anxin Mate Holdings Limited全部股權，有助于本集團擴展産品業務。

2011

 本集團于十壹月收購煜宏投資有限公司及其全資子公司深圳市豪威未來科技有限公司全部股權，拓展本集團産品業務。

 本集團正式更名爲“中國安芯控股有限公司”。

 集團調整發展戰略，集中資源發展高科技智能安防預警系統業務，退出藥品的制造和銷售業務。

 本集團于十月收購了祥鷹有限公司，其間接持有深圳市安芯數字發展有限公司的所有權益，此舉標致集團進軍國內安防市場。
