Intelligent Safety System, namely ISS system, is a powerful security system consisting of intelligent analysis technology and smart security device. It filters the invalid information intelligently, so that personnel can receive the significant information. Thus, ISS system can predict the safety hazards in advance and largely promote the working efficiency of security personnel, to keep the target job safe.

iVAS, a particular advanced video analysis system detecting both individuals and objects in the video images in real time, is newly developed on the basis of Israeli advanced technology which Anxin-China acquired from Mate, an Israeli company, in 2012.iVAS analyzes all surveillance images,–only records related videos and sends alerts as soon as any individuals or objects violate a preset rule in video images.Such an improved system changes the traditional video-checking model into a real-time 24/7 surveillance model, or in another saying, it combines the pre-incident alerting, mid-incident treatmentand post-incident conclusion altogether in order to realize the complete surveillance process, presenting the safe defense more than a passive process.

智慧安全系統（Intelligent Safety System，簡稱ISS系統），ISS系統是由智慧分析技術與智慧安防設備組成的功能強大的安全系統。ISS系統將無效的資訊進行智慧過濾，只將有意義的資訊提供給工作人員。從而極大的提高安全系統的工作效率。ISS系統提前預測安全隱患，對其有效控制以保障安全。

ISS擁有國際尖端的智慧系統——智慧視頻分析系統（Intelligent Video Analysis System，簡稱iVAS系統）。iVAS系統是中國安芯控股有限公司收購以色列企業,Mate Intelligent Video後，對其視頻分析技術進行升級與應用，開發出專門針對人與物體相關行為進行即時偵測、動態跟蹤的視頻分析系統。一改傳統監控系統現場查看與事後查證相結合的模式，升級為系統全天候監控模式，將“事先預警、事中處理、事後取證”相結合，事先“全過程監控”，真正起到“安全防範”的作用。
