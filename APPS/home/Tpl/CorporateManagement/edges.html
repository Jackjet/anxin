China’s current macro-policy environment is largely profitable for the development of this group’s both ISD and ISS businesses. With governmental guidance about promoting industrial safety production and public safety industries, Anxin-China will certainly take supports from numerous partners and stockholders, as well as other social opportunities, to develop its ISD system constantly in China’s security and environmental surveillance industries. Anxin-China always responds to governmental orders timely, and focuses on building the leading corporation in China’s security industry with the principle that your safety is our business.

我们紧跟国家战略步伐

国家安全生产监督管理总局（「安监总局」）在二零一二年九月发布的「一一四」号文件也强调，到二零一五年年底，安监总局的目标是完成全国省、市级大中型企业的监控联网工作。

响应国家号召，专注中国安防，中国安芯一直在努力。
